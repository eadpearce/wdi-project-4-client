What is colLAB?

ColLAB is where you can request fic for any fandom with a prompt and someone else can fill that prompt. Like an anon meme but more.

Searchable tags

Search for fills or prompts by tags. Easily filter by filled or unfilled prompts.

Ratings

Prompts can have a suggested rating so you can filter prompts depending on what you want to write. You can also filter fills by rating.

Comments

Unlike anon memes, comments here are separate from prompts and fills. No more confusion!
